# Keyhole TIG welding  and P+T welding for petrochemical machinery

**Category:** Blog  
**Article ID:** 69  
**Published:** 2024-08-11 01:07:49

<p>Keyhole TIG welding not only has advantages in speed over double gun composite P+T welding!</p>

<p><img alt="Keyhole TIG welding" height="904" src="/images/ktig/ktig-4.png" title="Keyhole TIG welding" width="1000"/></p>

<p>1 Concept;</p>

<p>1.1 K-TIG "Keyhole"TIG (keyhole or small hole)  also Keyhold TIG welding, not only has the beautiful cover effect of ordinary TIG welding, but also has the characteristics of super-penetrating power of plasma welding</p>

<p>1.2 P+T double gun composite welding: Plasma plasma welding gun and TIG welding gun are fixed into a welding gun, to realize plasma-TIG composite welding to make full use of the advantages of plasma arc welding and TIG welding methods;</p>

<p>2.1 P+T composite welding</p>

<p>In recent years, composite welding technology has been a research hotspot in the field of welding. Compared with the common single arc welding, the composite welding technology can effectively improve the welding quality and efficiency</p>

<p>+ High efficiency and high quality plasma and TIG simultaneous welding process method, the use of plasma good hole penetration ability, to ensure the single-side welding two-sided forming at the same time, as far as possible to improve the welding speed, the free arc through TIG welding has a good cap capacity, and then with the appropriate amount of filling metal, to achieve the positive forming effect;</p>

<p>+ Double gun welding is about 1.3 times the efficiency of single gun welding;</p>

<p>+ Suitable for long weld welding and high quality welding, mainly used in stainless steel, titanium alloy 3~10mm welding</p>

<p><img alt="Keyhole TIG welding" class="float-none" height="928" src="/images/ktig/ktig-2.png" title="Keyhole TIG welding" width="1079"/></p>





<p>P+T welding main process advantages:</p>

<p>The plasma arc penetration ability is strong, the plate thickness below 8mm does not need to be bevelled, greatly reducing the preparation time before welding and reducing the amount of filler wire.</p>

<p>② Welding speed can be significantly improved, double gun welding at the same time, high production efficiency.</p>

<p>③ Can be single-sided welding double-sided forming, stable to obtain a complete regular full welding rust weld, the appearance of good forming. ④ Meet 100% X-ray inspection requirements.</p>

<p>⑤ arc energy concentration, welding heat affected zone is small, welding deformation is small.</p>

<p>The welding heat input is small, the welding heat input is small, the weld metal grain size is small compared with submerged arc welding, and the joint performance is excellent.</p>

<p>⑦ Welding arc has good controllability and adjustability.</p>

<p>⑧ can be widely used in the welding of strain strengthened vessels, and the joint performance is excellent.</p>

<p>⑨ The welding environment is clean, and the labor intensity is greatly reduced</p>
