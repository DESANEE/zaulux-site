Investing it in the production line can improve production efficiency and bring higher economic benefits to enterprises.<br/>  As a walking auxiliary mechanism for<strong><a href="welding-machine/robot"> Robots</a></strong>, the ground track has also rapidly developed and is widely used in some complex spatial and large workpiece situations. The robot track is mainly used to drive <em>welding robot t</em>o move along designated routes, expand the robot's operating radius, expand the robot's usage range and functions, further improve the efficiency of robot use, reduce robot usage costs, and achieve comprehensive automated production.</h2>
