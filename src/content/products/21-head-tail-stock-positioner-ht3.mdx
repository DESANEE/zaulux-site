# Head Tail stock positioner HT3 for pipe welding

**Category:** positioner  
**Article ID:** 21  
**Published:** 2024-08-07 11:41:19

<p><strong><strong>Head Tail stock positioner HT3</strong></strong> is one machine which main apply for engineering machinery; our <strong><strong>Welding positioner </strong></strong>is mainly used to placed the work piece in the best position for welding and assembling with the work bench elevating, turning, tilting , its series includes tilting, <a href="index.php/products/positioner-welding">head  tail stocks positioner</a> ,elevating ,and double turning positioner, cradle type. The positioner can be supplied for interfacing with robot or welding manipulators ,to realize the welding automation. At the same time we can design and customize various type of special positioner according to the technological requirement and different work piece given by the customer.</p>

<p><strong>Head Tail stock positioner HT3</strong><strong> </strong>is used in  <a href="http://www.desanee.com" rel="noopener" target="_blank">engineering machinery</a>, wind power equipment, construction &amp; bridge, shipbuilding, food &amp; pharmaceutical and other industries</p>
