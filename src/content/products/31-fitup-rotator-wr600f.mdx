# Fitup Rotator WR600F for wind tower build line

**Category:** turning rolls  
**Article ID:** 31  
**Published:** 2024-08-10 03:28:43

<p><strong><strong><em>Wind Fitup Rotator WR600F</em> </strong></strong>is a kind of our advanced welding equipment , which is be researched and manufactured absorbing domestic and international advanced equipment .<strong><em><a href="welding-machine/turning-rolls" rel="noopener" target="_blank">Wind Fitup Rotator WR600F</a></em></strong> is mainly used for pipe, containers, boilers, pressure vessels, oil tank and other cylinder’s assembling, burnishing and welding, can use together with column and boom and SAW system to realize inside and outside girth and longitudinal welding. Its series includes self aligning rotators (SAR), and conventional rotators (WR). Other customize have trolley tilting anti drift rotator； Accordingly this device has advantages of compact structure, small cubage, beautiful shape, lightweight, convenient operating and so on. The machine can assort all kinds of auto welding or manual welding, improving welding quality, lessening welding labor and improve productivity.</p>

<p>We can provide solutions to unique welding rotator according to the customer’s requirement</p>
