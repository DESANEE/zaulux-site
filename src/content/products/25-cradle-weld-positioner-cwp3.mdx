# Cradle Weld Positioner CWP3 for oil tank and engineering machinery

**Category:** positioner  
**Article ID:** 25  
**Published:** 2024-08-07 12:04:45

<p><strong><strong><em>Cradle Weld Positioner CWP3</em></strong></strong> is mainly used to placed the work piece in the best position for welding and assembling with the work bench elevating, turning, tilting , its series includes tilting, head and tail stocks ,elevating ,and double turning positioner, cradle type. <strong><em><a href="welding-machine/positioner-welding">Cradle Weld Positioner CWP3</a></em></strong>  can be supplied for interfacing with robot or welding manipulators ,to realize the welding automation. At the same time we can design and customize various type of special positioner according to the technological requirement and different work piece given by the customer.</p>

<p><img alt="cradle weld positioner " height="619" src="/images/instead%20of/CWP-1.png" title="cradle weld positioner " width="2075"/></p>

<p><img alt="cradle weld positioner " height="402" src="/images/wp/CWP-001.jpg" title="cradle weld positioner " width="1000"/></p>



<p>Dwp type elevating welding <a href="http://www.desanee.cn">positioner </a>is mainly used for solving problems of high position, also can weld seam provide low installation position, make the workpiece clamp and fix more easily or double , including single revolve and double revolve frame , can make the work piece on better welding position.</p>

<p>+ Ball screw (or chain), with security nut, safe and reliable.</p>

<p>+ High strength, cooperate, inverter, no impact on the smooth.</p>

<p>+ Using electrical synchronous on elevating action ,  easily fast installation, commissioning, maintenance convenience.</p>
