# Chain Rotator CWR20 for wind power equipment

**Category:** turning rolls  
**Article ID:** 30  
**Published:** 2024-08-10 03:25:39

<p><strong><strong><em>Chain Rotator CWR20</em></strong></strong> is a kind of our advanced welding equipment , which is be researched and manufactured absorbing domestic and international advanced equipment .<strong><em><a href="welding-applications/construction" rel="noopener" target="_blank" title="Chain Rotator CWR20">Chain Rotator CWR20</a></em></strong> is mainly used for pipe, containers, boilers, pressure vessels, oil tank and other cylinder’s assembling, burnishing and welding, can use together with column and boom and SAW system to realize inside and outside girth and longitudinal welding. Its series includes self aligning rotators (SAR), and conventional rotators (WR). Other customize have trolley tilting anti drift rotator； Accordingly this device has advantages of compact structure, small cubage, beautiful shape, lightweight, convenient operating and so on. The machine can assort all kinds of auto welding or manual welding, improving welding quality, lessening welding labor and improve productivity.</p>

<p>We can provide solutions to unique welding rotator according to the customer’s requirement.E.G chian type  also call Chain rotator CWR20  for thin wall tank.</p>

<p><img alt="chain rotator" height="900" src="/images/wr/12wr00-37.jpg" title="chain rotator" width="1200"/></p>

<p><strong><strong>      Chain Rotator CWR20</strong></strong><strong> </strong>(welding rorator) is used in petrochemical machinery, engineering machinery, wind power equipment, <a href="welding-applications/construction" rel="noopener" target="_blank" title="WELDING ROTATOR">construction &amp; bridge</a>, shipbuilding, food &amp; pharmaceutical and other industries</p>
