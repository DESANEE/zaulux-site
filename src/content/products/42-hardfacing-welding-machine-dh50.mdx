# Hardfacing welding Machine DH50 for  engineering machinery

**Category:** omni manipulator  
**Article ID:** 42  
**Published:** 2024-08-10 05:58:32

<h2><strong><strong><em><a href="https://www.desanee.cn/s-Hardfacing%20welding%20Machine%20DH50?sort=6d">Hardfacing welding Machine DH50 </a></em></strong></strong></h2>

<h2><strong><strong>1.Summarize: </strong></strong></h2>

<p>This machine mainly useds for the hot roller or other cylinderses the surface of repair welding after wear away, welding method is the automatic welding of submerge arc (SAW or FCAW) .The work piece is tighted by  four claw chuck , this machine contain operation convenience, the structure is simple and adjusts quick etc. advantage, is the essential equipments of the house of professional factory of hardfacing welding.</p>

<p><img alt="Hardfacing welding" height="936" src="/images/instead%20of/DH10-0.png" title="Hardfacing welding" width="1726"/></p>

<h2><strong><strong>2. Function characteristics:</strong></strong></h2>

<p>     Dh50 type machine is a main process equipment used to hardfacing welding hot roller ,its framework is cantilever , on it fixed traverse rail and drag board ,and its travel speed adopted SPWM AC or DC control way, which is widespread adoption in automation realm currently. Its function stability credibility, the usage convenience, its speed as 2-230 mm/ min, can carry on regulate according to the request of the welding within the allow range ;We can provide with single wire single arc arc welding head according to customers requirement , welding positioner is used to placed the work piece in the best position for welding. at the same time we can design and customize various type of special positioner according to the technological requirement and different work piece given by the customer.</p>

<p>Welding equipments adoption of this machine compares ZXG-1000 welding power supply, the machine head and the control box adoption MA-1000 have the credibility high, weld stable etc. characteristic.</p>

<p> <strong><strong>Hardfacing welding Machine DH50 </strong></strong><strong><strong> </strong></strong>(<a href="welding-machine/welding-manipulator">plasma welding</a>) is used in petrochemical machinery, engineering machinery, wind power equipment, construction &amp; bridge, shipbuilding, food &amp; pharmaceutical and other industries</p>

<h2><strong><strong>3.Technique parameter:</strong></strong></h2>

<p>Apply max diameter:  Φ1000 mm     </p>

<p>Apply length:      5000 mm        </p>

<p>Apply weight:      6 T              </p>

<p>The turn-over center high:    800mm</p>

<p>Principal axis power:       0.75 KW</p>

<p>principal axis turning speed:  0.1-1rpm</p>

<p>Horizontal motor power:     40W</p>

<p>Horizontal speed:      2--30 mm/ min</p>

<p>Welding current :      200-1000A</p>

<p>Apply wire diameter :  Φ2.4.0-4.0 mm</p>
