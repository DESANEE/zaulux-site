# Elvating welding positioner for engineering machinery

**Category:** positioner  
**Article ID:** 20  
**Published:** 2024-08-07 11:34:04

<p><strong><strong> welding positioner</strong></strong><strong><strong> </strong></strong>is mainly used to placed the work piece in the best position for welding and assembling with the work bench elevating, turning, tilting , its series includes tilting, head and tail stocks ,elevating ,and double turning positioner, cradle type. The positioner can be supplied for interfacing with robot or welding manipulators ,to realize the welding automation. At the same time we can design and customize various type of special welding positioner according to the technological requirement and different work piece given by the customer.</p>

<h2><strong><strong> welding positioner</strong></strong>  and <a href="welding-applications/perochemial">double turning positioner </a>  is used in <strong><strong><a href="welding-applications/perochemial">petrochemical machinery</a></strong></strong>, engineering machinery, <a href="products/positioner">wind power equipment</a>, bridge construction, shipbuilding, food and pharmaceutical industries ,you can find other type welding positioner .</h2>
