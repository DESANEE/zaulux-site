<p>   Solar controller also Photovoltaic power generation is based on the principle of photovoltaic effect, using solar cells to directly convert solar energy into electrical energy. Regardless of whether it is used independently or connected to the grid for power generation, photovoltaic power generation systems are mainly composed of solar panels (components), controllers and inverters. They are mainly composed of electronic components, but do not involve mechanical components.<br/>     Therefore, photovoltaic power generation equipment is extremely refined, reliable, stable, long-lived, and easy to install and maintain. In theory, photovoltaic power generation technology can be used in any occasion where power is needed, from spacecraft to household power sources, as large as megawatt power stations, as small as toys, and photovoltaic power sources can be ubiquitous.</p>
